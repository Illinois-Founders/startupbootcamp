About

Startup Bootcamp II is the second cohort of an 8 week startup accelerator at the University of Illinois. Our goal is to help Illinois students develop fundamental entrepreneurial skills while building their startup.


During the first half of the program teams will work on developing a vision for their products and refining their ideas. Half-way through the program each team will give their first pitch during which they will receive feedback from the mentors. Following this they will continue to work on their ideas and receive more technical workshops on financing, legal, and product. At the culmination of the program we will host Demo Day where the teams give full length pitches to judges and the community.
